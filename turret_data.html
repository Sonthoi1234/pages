Turret data

This is where you can find extra information about all the turrets in our Tower Defense Game "extension", mostly about technical stats, how it works, what special ability it has and more.
The game provides just about enough for them, but there isn't enough space for extra questions to be answered.
Some turrets mentioned here that are not in the game yet may be added within the upcoming updates.
For turrets with special upgrades & abilities, visit the Special upgrades page.

Basic Damage: 1
Speed: 0.8
Range: 120
Cost: $50		 No A single barrel turret.
Probably your kindest friend early-game.
Has a low firerate but decent damage. It's range is enough to cover a good proportion of the map.
The cheap cost makes it versatile for any situation (except late-game).
Double Damage: 1
Speed: 0.4
Range: 100
Cost: $100		 No A double barrel turret.
The best friend for literally any purposes.
Has a small range but can shoot faster. Double the cost of a Basic, but far better than what you think.
Laser Damage: 2
Speed: 0.3
Range: 120
Cost: $150		 No A laser turret, having an electrical coil warping around it (for aesthetic purposes).
Can fire lasers going infinitely far away, able to hit multiple enemies at once.
Good for large batches of enemies.
Triple Damage: 1
Speed: 0.5
Range: 110
Cost: $225		 No A triple barrel turret.
Though having a small range, it's speed will compensate for that.
A slightly cheaper replacement for Wind turret, which can shoot orders of magnitude faster.
Red Laser Damage: 3
Speed: 0.5
Range: 110
Cost: $360		 No A laser turret.
Similar design to Laser turret, but everything about it is just red.
The coil warping around three times indicates a slower charging time.
Has the highest base damage of all turrets in-game, and can be more powerful in large amounts.

Range: 100

Earth Damage: 1
Speed: 0.6
Range: 130
Cost: $500		 Yes A double barrel turret.

Electric Damage: 1
Speed: 0.8
Range: 135
Cost: $520		 Yes A single barrel turret.
Can strike the target enemy, dealing lightning damage.
Has a chance to stun the target, stopping them from moving.
If you have the money, go for it. Especially good with high firerate.
Snow Damage: 1
Speed: 1.1
Range: 130
Cost: $500		 Yes A single barrel turret.
One of the slowest turrets, although it's abilities can save it from being totally useless.
Can freeze the target (doesn't stun them), slowing them and dealing snow damage.
Gear Damage: 1
Speed: 0.6
Range: 130
Cost: $700		 No A single barrel turret.
Its speed scales proportionally to the number on enemies on-screen.
You're better off placing some with max damage upgrade near the end of the pathway (since more enemies would show up, increasing its speed).
